The Best Movies According to Harshit

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Titanic

I like romantic movies and this is the greatest romantic movie of all time. Even in tragedy, someone can find love.

Avengers(2012)

The first avengers movie is has all the elements in it like the comedy, tragedy, love, the VFX and one of the things that I like it mostly for is the brightness of this movie. Most superhero movies are dull nowadays because of excessive CGI used.

Avengers: Infinity War

This movie has a great storyline and the direction is masterclass.
